who is nomads?

Nomads is a design studio founded by Gary Liu (me with that big smile on the left). I am a self taught digital designer who specializes in Web, Branding and UI design. Having lived and worked in SF/LA/NZ for the past 8 years, I have chased after waves, dreams and really good coffee - but what continues to keep me up working through the night, is telling compelling brand stories visually and designing product experiences that delight.
