It's time for a change

It's not uncommon to see designers put certain things on the back burner (often their website) when they have been too busy doing beautiful work for others. An accomplished interior designer, Veryan Laity approached us to help redesign her web portfolio to showcase her collection of work over the years. Over good coffee on a rainy day, it didn't take long for us to also uncover her willingness to rebrand her firm entirely, including the name itself - IK2 Design.

Though logical at the time (her first business was a furniture importer called IK Design), the name IK2 no longer reflected her artistic persona and continued passion as an interior designer. What was missing in her branding and website were both the sophistication and clarity that characterize the work that she curates for her own clients. She knew what she wanted but she hadn't been able to communicate it visually in digital form. So we gathered some of her inspirations and began to formulate a new visual identity.

Showcase the work

Veryan had a simple objective: showcase her work. The basic grid structure allows her work to take prominence, and a short introduction provides a personal touch to each project. Simple navigations including a "next project" button allows users to flow from one project to another seamlessly and an expanded gallery view showcases her work without any distractions. We also added a new footer section that includes an open form for emails along with links to her social media accounts and a testimonial blurb to highlight her accomplishment.
